## 📦 Dataset Used
-Source: Kaggle Credit Card Fraud Detection

### Description:

-284,807 transactions

-492 fraud cases (≈0.17%)

-Features: 30 columns including:

-Time, Amount (raw)

-V1 to V28 (PCA-transformed features)

-Class: 1 = Fraud, 0 = Not Fraud
